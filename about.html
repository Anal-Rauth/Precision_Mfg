About Us

Who We Are

Precision Manufacturing Co. is a full-service industrial manufacturing partner specializing in high-accuracy machining, fabrication, and assembly solutions.

Built on Engineering Excellence

Since our inception, we have focused on one core principle: consistent, repeatable quality. Our facilities are equipped with advanced CNC machines, automated inspection systems, and a highly trained workforce that understands the demands of modern production environments.

We serve customers across automotive, aerospace, heavy engineering, packaging, material handling, and custom machinery segments. From small precision components to turnkey systems, our team is capable of taking on complex engineering challenges and delivering reliable results.

Every project we undertake is backed by robust planning, strict process control, and detailed documentation. This allows our customers to scale confidently while maintaining safety, compliance, and uptime on the shop floor.

Our Direction

Mission & Vision

Clear goals guide every decision we make — from the technology we invest in to the way we support our customers on the shop floor.

Our Mission

To deliver high-precision, reliable manufacturing solutions that help our customers improve productivity, reduce downtime, and build safer, smarter industrial operations.

We do this by combining strong engineering fundamentals with disciplined execution, transparent communication, and long-term support.

Our Vision

To be the preferred manufacturing partner for industries worldwide, recognized for our ability to turn complex ideas into robust, production-ready solutions.

As technology evolves, we aim to lead in automation, digital integration, and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Core Values

Our work is anchored in integrity, accountability, and continuous improvement. We believe in building relationships based on trust, technical clarity, and measurable performance.

Every team member is encouraged to think like an engineer and act like an owner, ensuring we consistently deliver value to our customers.
